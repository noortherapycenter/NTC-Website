Job Description

As a Level II provider, you deliver EIDBI treatment under the supervision of a QSP. Your duties include:

  • Implement each client's Individual Treatment Plan (ITP) and deliver EIDBI intervention as assigned
  • Collect accurate session data and complete session notes the same day
  • Assist with family and caregiver training; participate in coordinated care conferences
  • Follow behavior intervention protocols and report progress or concerns to the QSP
  • Attend and document required observation and direction per your supervision plan
  • Maintain client confidentiality and a safe, engaging treatment environment

Qualifications: bachelor's degree in a related field plus 1,000 supervised clinical hours, RBT or BCaBA certification, or an approved alternate pathway with scheduled observation and direction (per DHS-7120E).
