Job Description

As QSP, you assume full professional responsibility for EIDBI services delivered under your supervision. Your duties include:

  • Clinically supervise Level I, II, and III providers, including required observation and direction
  • Develop, monitor, and update each assigned client's Individual Treatment Plan (ITP)
  • Coordinate CMDE evaluations and translate findings into treatment goals
  • Provide EIDBI intervention, family and caregiver training and counseling, and coordinated care conferences
  • Ensure treatment fidelity, review session data, and adjust programming based on client progress
  • Maintain timely, accurate clinical documentation and support authorization and billing compliance
  • Maintain active licensure/certification and update MHCP affiliations upon any change

Qualifications: licensed mental health professional, licensed behavior analyst (LBA/BCBA), or developmental/behavioral pediatrician; at least 2,000 hours of supervised clinical experience with ASD or related conditions (per DHS-7120C).

Conditions of Employment
