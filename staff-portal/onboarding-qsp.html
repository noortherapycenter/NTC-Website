QSP / BCBA — Qualified Supervising Professional

The QSP clinically supervises all EIDBI services and assumes full professional responsibility for services provided by Level I, II, and III providers. The agency cannot bill for services delivered without a QSP.

Qualification Requirements (DHS-7120C)

License — at least one of the following

  • Licensed mental health professional (Minn. Stat. §245.4871, subd. 27, cl. 1–6)
  • Licensed Behavior Analyst (LBA / BCBA under Minn. Stat. §148.99861, subd. 7)
  • Developmental or behavioral pediatrician

Experience

At least 2,000 hours of supervised clinical experience or training in examining or treating people with ASD or a related condition — or equivalent graduate-level coursework in ASD diagnostics, developmental and behavioral treatment strategies, and typical child development.

Employment & documentation

Must be an employee of the agency. Attach a copy of the current license to the DHS-7120C, and keep a signed copy on record for auditing.

MHCP Enrollment

Submit through MPSE. Upload the signed DHS-7120C with the Individual Provider Enrollment Application (DHS-4016), the DHS-4138, and proof of licenses through the MPSE Portal, or fax to 651-431-7462. QSPs must update affiliations immediately upon separation (DHS-3535). File the completed packet against the Employee File Checklist.
