Level I Provider

Advanced treatment provider. Delivers EIDBI intervention, provides observation and direction to Level II and III providers, and supports ITP development and progress monitoring under the supervision of a QSP.

Qualification Requirements (DHS-7120D)

Experience — all applicants

At least 2,000 hours of supervised clinical experience or training in examining or treating people with ASD or related conditions — or equivalent graduate coursework, or an equivalent combination.

Education / certification — at least one

  • Doctoral or master's degree in behavioral health, child development, or a related field
  • BCBA, BCBA-D, or Qualified Behavior Analyst (QBA)
  • Bachelor's degree in a related field plus a DHS-recognized advanced certification
  • BCaBA with 4,000 hours of supervised clinical experience

Alternate pathways

  • Currently enrolled in a qualifying master's program, with monthly QSP observation & direction until reaching 2,000 supervised hours
  • No master's degree: 6,000 hours of clinical early-intervention experience in the agency's treatment modality, a bachelor's degree in a related field, and completed Level III training

MHCP Enrollment

Submit through MPSE. Upload the signed DHS-7120D with the enrollment packet through the MPSE Portal or fax to 651-431-7462. The effective date on the form is the first date the provider can bill at Level I. File the completed packet against the Employee File Checklist.
